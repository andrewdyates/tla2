\author{Andrew Yates}

\begin{abstract}
We present complete formal verification of three C functions for h-index computation
on reverse-sorted integer arrays. Our solution addresses all 14 tasks of
VerifyThis 2026 Challenge~1, providing rigorous Hoare-logic proofs with
explicit loop invariants, termination arguments via well-founded orderings,
and correctness proofs establishing that each function computes the h-index
as specified. We verify safety (termination and absence of out-of-bounds access),
functional correctness, preservation of the sorted invariant under update, and
complexity bounds. Bonus tasks including a sorting routine, composition correctness,
and a generalized multi-citation update are also addressed.
\end{abstract}
